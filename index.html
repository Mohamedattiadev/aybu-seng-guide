About this guide

Single-page, unofficial student companion for AYBU Software Engineering — full curriculum, internship process, graduation requirements, faculty contacts, ASEC club. Track your progress, grades, and GNO right inside the page.

What you need to graduate

  • 240total ECTS over 8 semesters
  • 2.00minimum GPA (out of 4.00)
  • 50ECTS from SENG department electives
  • 6ECTS from Faculty / University electives
  • 2mandatory internships — SENG300 (after sem 4) + SENG400 (after sem 6)
  • 1graduation project — SENG401 + SENG402 (sem 7 & 8)
  • DDminimum grade in any prereq marked with *

Program: 100% English · 1 prep year + 4-year bachelor · Etlik campus.

Internship (Mandatory)

2 Mandatory InternshipsSENG300 Industrial Practice I (Semester 5) + SENG400 Industrial Practice II (Semester 7). Both required for graduation.

Official internship page

Required documents

  • Mandatory Internship Letter (e-signed)
  • Internship Acceptance Form (company signed)
  • Pre-Internship Control Form
  • GSS Declaration and Commitment Form
  • Internship Report (with daily summary)
  • Internship Result Document (signed by company engineer)
  • Post-Internship Control Form

Internship 1 — SENG300 0 / 10

Done in the summer after Semester 4 (counts toward Semester 5).

Process — before internship

  • Read AYBU Internship Directive.
  • Find a suitable workplace.
  • Give e-signed internship letter to the company.
  • Get signed acceptance form.
  • Submit documents to Internship Commission before deadline.
  • Request insurance document 1-2 business days before start.

Process — after internship

  • Write daily summary + general report.
  • Get workplace evaluation + company stamp on each page.
  • Seal documents in envelope with supervisor signature.
  • Spiral-bind the report and submit to the commission.

Internship 2 — SENG400 0 / 10

Done in the summer after Semester 6 (counts toward Semester 7).
